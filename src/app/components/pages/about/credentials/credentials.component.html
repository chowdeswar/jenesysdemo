Green Credentials

We passionately believe in and practice the idea of a green workspace. We are conscious of the finite natural resources on the planet and their steady depletion, and take care to reduce our collective carbon footprint by doing all we can to make ours a 'green' office.


These are some of the green initiatives we have undertaken so far :


  • We actively practice the three Rs - Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle, for our resources
  • We take care to only buy products that have low or no formaldehyde, trichloroethylene or benzene in them
  • We have plants that clean the air in our office
  • We ensure good ventilation to afford sufficient air exchange
  • We use only cleaning agents that are low in volatile organic content
  • We encourage carpooling, and provide incentives to employees using public transport and bicycles
  • Our workstations as well as furniture are made from eco-friendly materials that do not harm the environment
  • We perform regular waste recycling
  • We use only recycled stationery
